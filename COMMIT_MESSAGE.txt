feat: Initial release - GPLX v1.0 (Fairware License Framework)

GPLX = MIT + GPL + Fairness

Introducing "Fairware" - a new approach to open source licensing.
"Sustainable fairness through open exchange and mutual good faith."

- gplx.txt: Framework specification
- xbasic.txt: XBasic fairness framework (detailed)
- LICENSE-TEMPLATE.txt: Base terms (philosophy, good faith, enforcement)
- FAIRNESS-TEMPLATE.txt: Contribution tiers and caps
- README.md: Documentation with usage guide

Key features:
- Dual path: Open (GPL-style) or Closed (MIT-style)
- 6-tier structure (T1-T6) based on revenue
- ~10-30% of commercial license rates
- Caps to prevent hostage situations
- Exempt: non-profits, public education, government, startups, contributors
- Inflation review every 3 years
- Good faith clause for all parties

Philosophy:
- Awareness project, not extraction
- Protecting open source ecosystem
- Not disrupting commercial space
- Not demonizing corporations
- Corporations are not the enemy. Maintainer burnout is.

Fork encouraged - take the idea and build something greater.
